About My Tech Friend

Empowering the next generation of young tech innovators across the world.

Our Mission

My Tech Friend is a global initiative designed to connect young innovators from different countries and help them develop digital and technical skills early. We believe children should have access to modern technology education including coding, robotics, artificial intelligence and creative digital tools.

What We Teach

💻 Coding

Kids learn programming languages such as HTML, JavaScript and Python.

🤖 Robotics

Students design and build robotics projects using Arduino and other tools.

🧠 Artificial Intelligence

Young innovators explore AI, machine learning and automation.

🎨 Digital Creativity

Designing apps, websites, games and creative digital experiences.
