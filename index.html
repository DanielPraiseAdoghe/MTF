About Us

My Tech Friend is a global initiative dedicated to nurturing young tech talents. We connect children from diverse backgrounds and provide them with the tools, mentorship, and resources they need to excel in technology.


Our program focuses on:


  • Coding and Programming
  • Robotics and AI
  • Digital Creativity
  • Problem Solving
